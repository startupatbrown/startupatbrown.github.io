Event Details for Participating Companies

Dear potential sponsor,

Brown CS is one of the nation's top computer science programs, and is well known for its talented, creative, and diverse students. This spring, we’re back with the sixth Startup@Brown, a day-long event aimed at connecting top students with amazing startups. Sponsor companies that come to recruit and students alike rave about Startup@Brown so we've kept it going, and improving.

Our mission is simple:

Give startups effective access to recruiting top talent and inspire students about startup culture.

Requirements

Participating in Startup@Brown is simple.

Expect to send at least 2 company representatives for the duration of the event, ideally one serving in a product role. Recruiters, engineers, and designers are welcome.

Participation involves a sponsorship of $400.
