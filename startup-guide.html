Startup Guide to Startup@Brown

Details for sponsors and startups

Dear participating company,

We hope this page is helpful as a guide for Startup@Brown. Don't hesitate to contact us if you have any further questions.

The event is based on a simple purpose:

Meet great talent, share your stories, and inspire students to get involved with startups.

Please keep in mind that the representatives you send to Startup@Brown are essential to accomplishing this mission.

Talk Formats

Workshops

A leader, engineer, or designer will teach a prepared 45-min workshop/talk to up to 40 students with 10 minutes for Q&A at the end.

The workshops aren't necessarily interactive and they can range from "what does it mean to be a product manager" to "what is social entrepreneurship" to tech talks from engineers.

Small-group Conversations

A startup representative and up to 15 students will sit in a circle and the students students can ask questions and hear stories from the leader for 40 mins.
